AI Times is comming, To be, or not to be that is the question

发布于：2023-02-18 14:00   标签分类： life

目前AI发展相当火热，ChatGPT现在发展到令人惊讶的程度，尽管有的时候他的回答并不一定准确，但是他对于对话的理解是近乎准确的。有很多人用它来写文章，写作业，写代码。AI代替大部分人的时代也许不远了，到时候会有大量的人失业，毕竟机器的费用总体是比人便宜的。这让我感到后背发凉。 每次巨大技术进步的同时，会淘汰一部分人，然后把财富转移到另一部分人手里。所以是利用AI发展自己的能力，还是固步自封被AI取代，这是一个选择。

Recently AI is developing very quick, ChatGPT is scarely good. Authougth, it’s answer is not awalys correct. But it’s comperhison is nearly right. A lot of people use it writing article, doing homework, and even codding. AI will replace human, a lot of people will lose their job. Cause AI is cheaper than human. This is worry me. While the technology is developing, there will cause some people lose their job, and the money flow to other people’s pocket. So using AI as a tool to develop yourself, to help making money, or just do nothing to wait AI replace you. It is a choice.

The development of AI is currently quite hot, with ChatGPT developing to an astonishing degree. Although its answers are not always accurate, its understanding of conversations is almost spot-on. Many people use it to write articles, assignments, and codes. The era of AI replacing most people may not be far away, which will lead to massive unemployment since machines are generally cheaper than humans. This makes me feel a chill down my spine. Every time a great technological breakthrough occurs, some people are eliminated and the wealth is transferred to another group. So it is a choice between taking advantage of AI development to enhance oneself’s capabilities, or standing still and being replaced by AI.
